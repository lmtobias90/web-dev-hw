Member Dues Exemption

Some of our community members are exempt from paying monthly dues. If any of these exemptions apply to you, be sure to let us know when you come sign up for a membership!

  • Elders (60+)
  • Current FPCC Board Memberships
  • Under 18 years old
  • FPCC current college students (6+ credits)
  • Diabetics
