KATE ROCHE'S SPECIAL - SOURDOUGH BREAD

INGREDIENTS
  • 250gAll-Purpose Flour
  • 250gBread Flour
  • 400glukewarm water
  • 75gActivated/fed sourdough starter
  • 10gSalt

SUPPLIES/EQUIPMENT
  • Digital kitchen scale
  • Silicone mixing spoon
  • 3-5qt Dutch oven
  • 1 clean wide-mouth jar
  • Dough scraper (optional, but recommended)
  • Banneton proofing bowl (optional)

DIRECTIONS
  1. Refresh Your Starter
    • Take starter out of fridge and let come down to room temp. At least 2-3 hours or overnight.
  2. Mix Your Dough
